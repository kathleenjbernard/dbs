About the Owner

Death By Sugar is owned and operated by Kira Bernard in her home kitchen. Kira is a Registered Nurse at a local children's hospital and operates Death By Sugar as a passion project. A self-described Hufflepuff, she began baking for coworkers as a way to increase morale and decrease the stress that comes with working in healthcare.

From an early age, Kira has identified with goth subculture and especially enjoys creating cakes with spooky motifs, but most of all, creating cakes that make people smile.
